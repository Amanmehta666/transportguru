Who We Are

Founded in 2020, TransportGuru is an Australian-based logistics provider specializing in innovative, safe, and reliable transport solutions. We bridge the gap between businesses and their customers through timely and secure delivery of goods.

Our Mission

To deliver trust, reliability, and speed in every shipment, empowering businesses to grow without logistical limits.

Our Vision

To become Australia’s most preferred and tech-driven logistics brand.

Our Journey

2020

Company founded in Melbourne with just 2 trucks and 5 employees.

2022

Expanded to Sydney and Brisbane. Added GPS and automation to our fleet.

2024

Crossed 1,000 clients and introduced AI-based route optimization.
